Directions
Pico De Gallo
  1. Combine all ingredients into a bowl.
  2. Add juice from half of your lime.
  3. Add salt (to your taste).
  4. Mix well.
Guacamole
  1. Take your two avocados into a bowl and mush until satisfied.
  2. Add your onion, cilantro, garlic powder and salt (both to taste).
  3. Add juice from your lime.
  4. Mix well.
Place both Pico and Guac into the fridge to cool.
Potatoes
  1. Preheat oven to 425°.
  2. Wash and dry your potatoes and chop into small cubes.
  3. In a large bowl with your potatoes, add olive oil and your taco seasoning (along with any perferred seasonings).
  4. Mix well.
  5. Lay out your seasoned potatoes onto a baking sheet and place into the oven for 30-35 minutes.
  6. Once potatoes are out of the oven, wait for them to cool for a few minutes before removing them from the baking sheet.
Ground Beef
  1. On a large skillet with medium high heat, add a bit of olive oil.
  2. Add your ground beef and break it up in the pan.
  3. After a 2 minutes, add your taco seasoning (and any other perferred seasonings).
  4. Mix well and add a small bit of water for extra moisture.
  5. Cook for 8-10 minutes, mixing often until cooked to your liking.
  6. OPTIONAL: While mixing, add a bit of your shredded cheese.
Combine all sections into a bowl or on a tortilla and enjoy!
 OPTIONAL: Add cheese and sour cream on top.
