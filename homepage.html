Delicious Delicacies

Teriyaki Chicken Fried Rice

Fried rice is a classic in any culture. Add in some teriyaki chicken and you're all set to be amazed. Be prepared to sit back and enjoy the process of this easy recipe! It tastes great and isn't mentally taxing to make! Enjoy! :)

Loaded Taco / Taco Bowl

This quick and easy recipe is perfect for any lunch or dinner and you could even add an egg for breakfast! Both healthy and packed with flavor! Find yourself enjoying in a bowl or on a tortilla. Delicious both ways! Enjoy! :)

Cinnamon Rolls

A sweet, soft pastry made from a rolled dough filled with a cinnamon-sugar mixture, often topped with icing or cream cheese glaze.

Brownies

A rich, chocolatey dessert bar with a dense, fudgy or cake-like texture, often enjoyed plain or with mix-ins like nuts or chocolate chips.

Steamed Salmon

A fast and simple traditional cantonese dish that feeds 4 people. This dish brings out the delicate texture of salmon and is usually served with rice. It can be served for dinner or on the holidays.

Crêpe Cake

A simple cake made from many layers of crepes that can be enjoyed in any occasion. With its simple design, changes can be made to personalize it easily just by adding or taking away toppings. It's light, fluffy, and refreshing, so give it a try!
