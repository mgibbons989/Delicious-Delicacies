Teriyaki Chicken Fried Rice

 Carbs: 58g

Ingredients














Directions
  1. Dice your chicken thighs
  2. In a large bowl, add your diced chicken thighs, soy sauce, avocado oil, garlic, and ginger along with any other perferred seasonings.
  3. Mix and let marinate for 30 minutes to an hour.
  4. Heat your skillet to medium high heat and add a bit of avocado oil.
  5. Add your chicken thighs to the skillet and cook until golden brown and fully cooked. Remove and set aside.
  6. In the same skillet, add a bit more avocado oil.
  7. Add in yellow and green onion and sautee for 2-3 minutes.
  8. Add in minced garlic and mixed vegetables.
  9. Cook for 1 minute, mixing well so it doesn't stick to the bottom of the pan.
  10. Move everything to one side and add your eggs.
  11. Once the eggs are cooked and scrambled, mix everything well.
  12. Once mixed, add your rice.
  13. Add your soy sauce, sesame oil, and your butter and mix well.
  14. Add in your diced chicken thighs and mix again.
Serve and enjoy!
